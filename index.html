Stray Kids is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment through the 2017 reality show of the same name. The group is composed of eight members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. Originally a nine-piece group, member Woojin left due to undisclosed personal reasons in October 2019.

Christopher Bang Chan was born on October 3, 1997 in Seoul, South Korea. He is the leader, producer, vocalist and dancer of the group Stray Kids.

Lee Minho was born on October 25, 1998 in Gimpo, South Korea. He is the main dancer, vocalist and visual of the group Stray Kids.

Seo Changbin was born on August 11, 1999 in Yongin, South Korea. He is the main rapper and producer of the group Stray Kids.

Hwang Hyunjin was born on March 20, 2000 in Seoul, South Korea. He is the main dancer, rapper and visual of the group Stray Kids.

Han Jisung was born on September 14, 2000 in Incheon, South Korea. He is the main rapper, vocalist and producer of the group Stray Kids.

Felix Lee (Lee Yongbok) was born on September 15, 2000 in Sydney, Australia. He is the main dancer, rapper and visual of the group Stray Kids.

Kim Seungmin was born on September 22, 2000 in Seoul, South Korea. He is the main vocalist of the group Stray Kids.

Yang Jeongin was born on February 8, 2001 in Busan, South Korea. He is the vocalist and maknae of the group Stray Kids.
